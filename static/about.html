About InkCoverage.app

What is it?

InkCoverage.app is a free, browser-based tool that calculates ink coverage percentages for PDF files — by channel, by page, and over any selected area. No installation. No registration. No file retention.

What does it do?

Upload a PDF, pick a page, optionally draw a crop rectangle over the area you care about, and hit Analyze. Within seconds you get the ink coverage percentage for each separation in the file: Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black, and any spot or special colors. You can step through every page individually or run a full document batch analysis in one click. Results can be copied to the clipboard or exported as a CSV.

Who is it for?

  • Prepress operators verifying ink load before sending a job to press
  • Print buyers and estimators calculating ink consumption and job cost
  • Graphic designers checking whether their artwork is within press specifications
  • Packaging engineers working with flexo, offset, or digital printing
  • Anyone in print production who needs a quick, honest answer to "how much ink is on this page?"

Most professional tools that do this are expensive, bundled inside larger prepress suites, or require sending your files to a third-party cloud. InkCoverage.app does one thing, does it well, and gets out of your way.

Why does it exist?

It was built out of a real need. Working in the printing industry, a lightweight tool was needed to quickly check ink coverage on incoming PDF files — something that didn't require launching a full prepress application just to get a number. Nothing satisfying existed for free. So it was built, and then made available to everyone who has the same problem.

How does it work?

Files are processed server-side using Ghostscript — the industry-standard open-source PostScript and PDF interpreter — with a Python backend managing the analysis pipeline. Each channel is separated and measured independently. Your file is automatically deleted from the server within 10 minutes of upload and is never shared, stored, or used for any other purpose.
